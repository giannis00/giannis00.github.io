A neural surrogate model that predicts the optimal signal-to-noise ratio of binary black hole mergers from IMRPhenomXPHM waveforms over an 11-dimensional effective parameter space on Advanced LIGO. Achieves 99.29% post-calibration accuracy at 1M samples (R^2 = 0.9998). Joint work with Prof. Tefas and Prof. Stergioulas.
