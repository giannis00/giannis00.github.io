A 1D-ResNet classifier (~60K parameters) trained with three rounds of hard-negative mining on ~130,798 TESS-SPOC light curves across 102 sectors, followed by a recall-first matched-filter survey. Final result: a null detection with an upper limit f_occ < 5.6e-5 (95% CL).
