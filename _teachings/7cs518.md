## Overview

This postgraduate module covers the core methods of natural language processing, from statistical language models and n-gram approaches to neural architectures designed for sequential data. Students examine the theoretical foundations and practical implementation of models for text understanding and generation.

## Topics

- Statistical language models and n-grams
- Text preprocessing and feature extraction
- Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)
- Long Short-Term Memory networks (LSTMs)
- Sequence-to-sequence modelling
- Introduction to transformer-based language models
