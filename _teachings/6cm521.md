## Overview

This module surveys the frontiers of artificial intelligence and data science, combining hands-on Python programming with an exploration of modern machine learning and deep learning techniques. Topics progress from classical ML through deep neural networks, explainable AI, reinforcement learning, and large generative models, with practical lab sessions throughout.

## Topics

- Python for data science: NumPy, pandas, and visualisation
- Supervised learning: regression, classification, and decision trees
- Unsupervised learning: clustering and dimensionality reduction
- Artificial neural networks and deep learning
- Explainable AI (XAI)
- Reinforcement learning
- Generative AI and large language models
- Fine-tuning with LoRA
