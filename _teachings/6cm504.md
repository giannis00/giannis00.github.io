## Overview

This module introduces the formal modelling and verification of concurrent systems using Communicating Sequential Processes (CSP) and the FDR4 model checker. Students specify, analyse, and verify system properties such as deadlock-freedom and trace refinement in realistic concurrent scenarios.

## Topics

- CSP: syntax, semantics, and process primitives
- Prefixing, choice, and parallel composition
- Trace, failures, and divergences semantic models
- Deadlock and livelock analysis
- Refinement checking with FDR4
- Case studies in concurrent system verification
