## Overview

This postgraduate module develops the analytical thinking and technical skills needed to extract value from business data. Students progress from data management and visualisation through descriptive, diagnostic, and predictive analytics, with hands-on work in SAS and Python. The module concludes with topics in classification, clustering, and the ethical governance of data-driven decision-making.

## Topics

- Introduction to business analytics: scope, value, and the analytics lifecycle
- Business analytics in organisations: strategy, roles, and real-world applications
- Data management and pre-processing: quality, transformation, and preparation
- Data visualisation and communicating insight for business impact
- Descriptive and diagnostic analytics
- Reporting and advanced tabulation with SAS PROC TABULATE
- SQL for business analytics
- Predictive analytics and regression techniques
- Classification for business analytics
- Clustering and unsupervised learning in business contexts
- Ethics, governance, and responsible analytics
